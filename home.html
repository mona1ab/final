It originated in 1916 as Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke, a builder of aircraft engines, but assumed the name Bayerische Motoren Werke in July 1917 and began producing motorcycles in the 1920s. BMW entered the automobile business in 1928. The company's R32 motorcycle set a world speed record that was not broken until 1937.

• Our Achievements

Over the years, BMW has etched its mark with revered models like the BMW 3 Series, 5 Series, and 7 Series, setting new standards in engineering and technology. Beyond the road, BMW has also triumphed on the racing circuits, showcasing prowess in Formula 1, touring car championships, and endurance racing. Embracing the future, BMW has made significant strides in sustainable mobility, epitomized by their electric and hybrid offerings, underlining a dedication to innovation, corporate responsibility, and an enduring reputation for luxury and quality on a global scale.

• Who We Are?

BMW Group not only lies in its products and technology, but also in the company’s history, written by inventors, pioneers and brilliant designers. Today, the BMW Group, with more than 30 production and assembly facilities as well as a global sales network, is the world’s leading manufacturer of premium automobiles and motorcycles, and provider of premium financial and mobility services.
